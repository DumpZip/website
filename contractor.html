Onboard Your Drivers

Complete these 3 steps to see your drivers in the app.

Step 1: Your Invite Link
dumpzip.com/driver-setup.html

Tap anywhere to copy this link. Send this to all your drivers.

Step 2: Copy this Message
"We are moving to Dump Zip for scheduling. You must sign up and add your trucks here: dumpzip.com/driver-setup.html"

Tap to copy this message to your group chat.

Step 3: When are they ready?

A driver is not "Ready to Book" until they have completed these two items in their profile:

✓
Trucks Added (Axle/Bed type)
✓
Parking Location Set

Once they do this, they will automatically appear in your "Available Trucks" list in the app.
