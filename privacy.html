Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Feb 18, 2024

Dump Zip, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Dump Zip mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Note to Users: Dump Zip is a business-to-business logistical tool. By using the App, you acknowledge that certain data (such as truck location) must be shared with other users to facilitate bookings.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

Personal Data

  • Email address, Phone number
  • First and last name
  • Company Name and US DOT Number
  • Business Address, City, State, ZIP code

2. Location Data (Precise Geolocation)

To facilitate matching trucks to job sites, we collect Precise Location Data.

  • Truck Owners: Your truck's location allows Contractors to find you by proximity.
  • Contractors: Job site locations are shared with Truck Owners to facilitate routing.

3. Communication Data

We collect messages sent through our "Booking Messages" feature to assist in record-keeping and dispute resolution.

4. Account Deletion & User Rights

You may request the deletion of your account at any time. To do so, please use the "Delete Account" feature in the App settings or contact us at hello@dumpzip.com.
